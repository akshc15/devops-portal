Job Roles to Apply For
These are the realistic roles after 30 days. Apply for all of them — titles vary by company for the same work.
🥇 Junior DevOps Engineer
₹4–8 LPA · Entry level
Docker + K8s + CI/CD + Linux. Your exact skillset. Most common title for freshers entering DevOps.
LinkedIn · Naukri · Instahyre
☁️ Cloud Engineer (Junior)
₹4–9 LPA · Entry level
GCP/AWS + Terraform + Linux. Your GKE + Terraform work directly maps here. Cloud teams love IaC skills.
LinkedIn · Naukri · company career pages
🔧 Platform Engineer
₹5–10 LPA · Slightly broader
K8s + Helm + CI/CD + monitoring. More developer-facing than traditional DevOps. Growing fast in product companies.
LinkedIn · AngelList · startup job boards
🛡️ Site Reliability Engineer (SRE)
₹6–12 LPA · Reliability focus
Monitoring + K8s + incident response + SLOs. Your Prometheus/Grafana work is perfect here. Emphasise uptime and reliability.
Big tech, banks, product startups
🔄 Build & Release Engineer
₹4–7 LPA · CI/CD focus
GitHub Actions + Docker + Jenkins. Your full CI/CD pipeline is exactly what this role does. Strong fit for service companies.
Infosys · TCS · Wipro · HCL · Capgemini
🐳 Docker/K8s Admin
₹4–8 LPA · Ops-heavy
Pure container operations. Manage clusters, debug pods, handle deployments. Very common role in mid-size companies.
Naukri · Indeed · company referrals

🎯 APPLICATION STRATEGY — Apply Smart, Not Everywhere

Week 1 after Day 30: Apply to 10–15 roles on LinkedIn with "Easy Apply". Only roles that say "0–2 years" or "Fresher" or "Junior".
Week 2: Apply directly on company career pages — less competition than job boards.
Every application: Paste your github.io URL in every form that has an "optional website/portfolio" field. It gets opened.
Don't apply to: Roles requiring 3+ years. You'll get filtered before a human sees your resume.

Certifications — Which Ones Actually Help
In order of value for getting your first job. Start with free ones while job hunting.
🏅
Google Cloud ACE
₹12,000 one-time fee
Associate Cloud Engineer. Directly validates your GCP + Kubernetes + Terraform skills from this challenge. Most in-demand GCP cert for freshers.
⭐ Highest value — do this first
☸️
CKA — Certified Kubernetes Administrator
₹29,000 · valid 3 years
The gold standard K8s cert. Linux Foundation exam. Purely hands-on — no MCQs, you actually operate a cluster. Instantly credible with any recruiter.
⭐ Most respected DevOps cert
🎓
Google Cloud Skill Badges
FREE on Google Cloud Skills Boost
Hands-on labs on GCP. Complete "Deploy to Kubernetes in GCP" skill badge in 1–2 days. Adds verifiable badges to your LinkedIn. Start here while saving for CKA.
✓ Free — do this week 1
🐳
Docker Certified Associate (DCA)
₹16,000 · valid 2 years
Validates Docker expertise. Covers everything in Week 2 of the challenge plus more. Good for companies where Docker is the main focus vs K8s.
✓ Good if applying to Docker-heavy roles
🏗️
HashiCorp Terraform Associate
₹6,500 · valid 2 years
Cheapest meaningful cert. Validates your Terraform IaC skills. MCQ-based exam — easier than CKA. Good for roles at companies heavily using Terraform.
✓ Easy win, affordable
📜
Coursera / Udemy Certificates
₹499 – ₹1,499
Do NOT list these on your resume. They carry zero weight with employers — anyone can buy them. Use them to learn, not to credential. Real hands-on experience beats any Udemy cert.
✗ Skip — no value to recruiters
